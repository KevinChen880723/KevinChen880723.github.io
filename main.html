About Me

Hello, I'm an M.S. student at the electronic and computer engineering department of NTUST. In my undergraduate, I learned numerous skills related to information technology such as IoT, cloud computing, embedded systems, machine learning, deep learning, and lots of programming languages. In the graduate program, I'm doing deep learning research related to ADAS, which can make the self-driving car safer. Besides, I have two internship experiences in CKmates and ITRI.

Work Experience

In this periods, I studied AWS Well-Architected Framework and made an IoT project. I used AWS IoT Core, AWS Lambda, and AWS API Gateway to develop the backend while using HTML, CSS, and Javascript to develop the frontend.
During the internship in ITRI, I developed a monocular depth estimation model. I studied a paper called "Digging Into Self-Supervised Monocular Depth Estimation" and used PyTorch to implement the architectures proposed in the paper. After training the depth estimation model, I used the concept of triangle similarity to convert the prediction into the unit of meter.
