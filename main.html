About Me

Hello, I'm an M.S. student at the electronic engineering department of NTUST. In my undergraduate, I learned numerous skills related to information technology such as IoT, AWS, embedded systems, machine learning, deep learning, and experience in lots of programming languages. In the graduate program, I'm doing deep learning research related to ADAS, which can make the self-driving car safer. Besides, I have two internship experiences in CKmates and ITRI.

Work Experience

During the internship in CKmates, I studied AWS Well-Architected Framework and made an IoT project. I used AWS IoT Core, AWS Lambda, and AWS API Gateway to develop the backend while using HTML, CSS, and Javascript to develop the frontend.
